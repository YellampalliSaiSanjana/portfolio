About me

Who I am

I'm a Computer Science Engineering student at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amaravati, passionate about building systems that are both technically solid and genuinely useful.

From building real-time crowd monitoring with YOLOv3 to crafting recipe apps with YouTube integrations — I love working across the stack. I also do active ML research with a paper on TinyML-based intrusion detection for EV charging systems.
